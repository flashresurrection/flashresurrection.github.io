About flash resurrection

Flash Resurrection is a company dedicated to preserving and reviving Flash games and content. They understand the cultural and historical significance of Flash and aim to ensure that Flash-based games and interactive experiences continue to be accessible to audiences worldwide.

With a team of dedicated developers and enthusiasts, Flash Resurrection works to archive and maintain a collection of Flash games, applications, and animations. By doing so, they strive to keep this important part of internet history alive and provide an opportunity for future generations to explore and enjoy the creativity and innovation that Flash brought to the web.

Flash Resurrection actively seeks out Flash content from various sources, including user submissions and partnerships with developers and creators. They are committed to maintaining the integrity of the original content and provide a platform for users to play and interact with Flash games in a safe and secure environment.

If you have any questions or would like to contribute to our mission, please visit the flashresurrection GitHub repository at https://github.com/flashresurrection.
